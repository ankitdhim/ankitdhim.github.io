Ankit Dhiman

ankitd@iisc.ac.in

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. (ERP) student at IISc Bangalore, advised by Prof. Venkatesh Babu. Also, I work full-time at Samsung Research Institute - Bangalore, focusing on perception solutions.

My research expertise lies in Computer Vision, Deep Learning, Generative AI, and Image Processing. Specifically, my current work explores interesting applications using the 3D representations like 3D Gaussian Splatting (3DGS) and NeRF. I am also keenly interested in leveraging 3D vision and generative models to solve challenging tasks, such as reflection generation on a mirror.
